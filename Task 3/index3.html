My Favourite Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup carrot, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup capsicum, finely chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornflour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying and cooking


    • Instructions

  • Coat cauliflower florets, carrots, and capsicum in a mixture of all-purpose flour, cornflour, salt, and pepper.
  • Deep fry the coated vegetables until they turn golden brown. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Heat some oil in a pan and add minced garlic and ginger. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add soy sauce and a little water to make the sauce.
  • Add the fried vegetables to the sauce and toss them well.
  • Serve the Manchurian hot as an appetizer or with noodles or fried rice.

    Ingredients

  • 4-5 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, capsicum, etc.), boiled and mashed
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Butter for cooking
  • Salt to taste
  • Pav (buns) for serving


    • Instructions

  • Heat butter in a pan and add minced garlic and ginger. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add chopped onions and cook until they turn translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  • Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add the mashed vegetables and potatoes. Cook and mash them together until well combined.
  • Toast the pav (buns) with butter on a griddle until they are browned.
  • Serve the pav bhaji hot with toasted pav and garnish with chopped onions and a dollop of butter.
